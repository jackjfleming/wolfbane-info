Scapegoat

Traveler

If a player of your alignment is executed, you might be executed instead.

Examples

The good Scapegoat is in play. The Alpha Wolf is about to be executed. The Storyteller chooses for the Scapegoat to be executed instead. The Alpha Wolf lives, the Scapegoat dies, and the game continues.

The evil Scapegoat is in play. The Empath is about to be executed. The Storyteller chooses for the Scapegoat to be executed instead. The Empath lives and the Scapegoat dies.

Tips & Tricks (If Good)

If you're good: If you are good, you want to protect good players from execution. Try to figure out who the good players are and be ready to take their place if they are about to be executed.

If you're good: You can use your ability to save powerful Townsfolk like the Hunter or Mayor from execution.

If you're good: Be careful not to save evil players by accident. Make sure you know who is good before using your ability.

Tips & Tricks (If Evil)

If you're evil: If you are evil, you want to protect evil players from execution. This can help keep the Alpha Wolf alive longer.

If you're evil: You can coordinate with your evil team to know when to use your ability.

If you're evil: Be prepared to sacrifice yourself to save the Alpha Wolf or important Minions.
